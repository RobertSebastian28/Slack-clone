How do I start ?

  • You can click on the channels category and choose an active channel to post in.

  • You can click on the direct message category to send a direct message to a person.

  • You can also send pictures in any chat by clicking on the plus symbol in the lower left corner of the input window.

  • If you want to send emojis you can also do that in the input window. Click on the emoji symbol at the bottom left of the input window.
